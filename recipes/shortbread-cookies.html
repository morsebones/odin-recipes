Shortbread Cookies

Description

Delicious and easy, these cookies will not last long. They are inexpensive to make, and only require a few ingredients that are commonly found in a kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks (16 tablespoons, 1 cup, or 8 oz) plus 2 to 4 Tablespoons unsalted butter, softened

  • 1 cup powdered sugar

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour

  • 1/2 cup cornstarch

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • 1 Tablespoon Vanilla Extract

  • 36 Hershey Kisses, unwrapped
