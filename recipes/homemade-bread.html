Homemade Bread

Description

A simple recipe to create two, delicious loaves of bread without having to go to the store. Loaves will last up to five days in a closed container, or can be frozen for up to two months.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups warm water (105-115 degrees)
  • 1 Tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup honey or sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 Tablespoons canola or vegetable oil
  • 4 - 5 1/2 cups all-purpose or bread flour
