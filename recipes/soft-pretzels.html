Soft Pretzels

Description

This is a copycat recipe for Auntie Anne's famous soft pretzels. If you have been craving a nice savory, buttery pretzel, then this is the recipe for you. It does take a bit of work, and can be messy, so you have been warned.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (470ml) milk (I used 2%)

  • 1 1/2 Tbsp active dry yeast (2 packets)

  • 6 Tbsp (75g) packed light-brown sugar

  • 4 Tbsp (56g) butter, at room temperature

  • 4 1/2 cups (635g) all-purpose flour, plus an up to an additional 1/2 cup as needed

  • 2 tsp fine salt

  • 1/3 cup baking soda

  • 3 cups (710ml) warm water

  • coarse salt, to taste

  • 6 Tbsp (56g) butter, melted
